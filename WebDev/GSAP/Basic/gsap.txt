GSAP : GreenSock Animation Platform

Smooth scrolling : locomotive JS

gsap.to("name of the element", {object(key:value)})   //to the destination final
gsap.from("name of the element", {object(key:value)})   //to the source initial

Stagger can be used instead of train or timeline when u don't want to select every individual element

        - trigger to whom,
        - scroller on what
        - markers (for indications)
        - start and end positions 
        - scrub for smoothness from 0 to 5
